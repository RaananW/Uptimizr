# Vendored 3D models — attribution & licenses

The glTF models in this folder are bundled with the Uptimizr playground so the
demo scenes are fully self-contained (no network fetch, deterministic in E2E).
All models are sourced from the
[Khronos glTF Sample Assets](https://github.com/KhronosGroup/glTF-Sample-Assets)
repository and are commercial-use-clean (CC0 or CC BY 4.0). Models under
non-commercial (CC BY-NC) or non-CC licenses are intentionally **not** used.

## Updating

To refresh or add a model, download the `glTF-Binary` `.glb` from
[KhronosGroup/glTF-Sample-Assets](https://github.com/KhronosGroup/glTF-Sample-Assets/tree/main/Models),
prefer CC0/CC BY 4.0 only, and update the table above with its copyright line.

## What is Uptimizr?

Uptimizr is an open-source, self-hostable analytics platform for 3D scenes. Drop a connector into
your Babylon.js, three.js, PlayCanvas, react-three-fiber, or A-Frame app and it captures the
spatial signals flat web analytics can't — gaze, clicks, mesh interactions, performance — then
aggregates them into heatmaps, rankings, and full session replay.
